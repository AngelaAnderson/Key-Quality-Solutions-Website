Meet Your Consultant

A Global Leader in Pharmaceutical Consulting

Kenyatta Scott Oliphant

Founder of KQS

My name is Kenyatta Scott-Oliphant and I am the founder of Key Quality Solutions LLC, a consultancy that provides advice, guidance and strong leadership through active problem-solving, strategic planning and global cooperation across cross-functional teams. I have a bachelor’s degree in Biology with a minor in Chemistry and more than 15 years of professional experience in both the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. To ensure success for my clients, I draw on advanced knowledge and best practices in quality assurance, compliance management and process improvement. I have on-the-job experience with batch release, production operations, regulatory inspection and quality systems management. And I have demonstrated success with overseeing and completing multiple project initiatives designed to streamline operations and support company objectives.

I began my career working at Procter & Gamble’s Norwich Eaton Pharmaceuticals as a staff scientist testing OTC medication. Later, I joined Amgen and provided plant Quality Assurance support supervising a team responsible for batch release, environmental monitoring, validation and internal audits for the startup of a monoclonal antibody cell culture, fermentation, purification, formulation and fill/finish facility. A subsequent position within the cosmetics industry at Colgate-Palmolive allowed me to hone skills managing a team responsible for process validation, audits and inspection readiness. I then returned to biotechnology industry to work at Schering-Plough where I restructured and validated the company’s CMC submission publishing tool with an electronic document control system. I later transitioned to MedImmune, now AstraZeneca where I was responsible for quality system project management and global product complaint management.

My specialties include assisting healthcare startups with key tasks critical to their success, including implementation of compliant global electronic quality management systems. No matter the project, I bring a wealth of quality control knowledge and best practices to every product lifecycle. I welcome future opportunities to continually make a difference creating positive business outcomes.
