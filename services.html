Quality Management Systems

We know you want satisﬁed customers and sound long-term strategies. We’ll help you establish the best possible procedures to achieve them.

We’ll help you group your processes, policies, and procedures into a Quality Management System (QMS) that complies with both ICH 10, ISO 9001, and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMPs). We specialize in document management, change control, product complaints, risk management non-conformance and CAPA management. We offer support in interpretation of regulations, conducting gap analysis, developing a product plan, designing agile digital quality systems and training your employees.

Supplier Management

KQS puts you in full control of your company’s operations by identifying every opportunity to streamline and improve its processes on the supply end.

Supplier management ensures you obtain the highest standard of service from your suppliers, whether they are providing raw material or components, medical devices, outsourced testing, or contract manufacturing. We assist with quality agreement, supplier documents, and supplier certification. We’ll help you ensure that all quality agreements with suppliers support the needs of your business by offering seamless digital solutions for sharing and maintaining the security of your data.

Daily Quality Operations

We will assess your production and apply clinical trial studies to enhance your market distribution and commercial operations.

Quality has to be built into products. Quality ensures the consistency and reliability of delivering safe, pure, and effective products to patients. We’ll offer virtual or on-site services to help you oversee the product operation process. Our solutions allow you to approve raw material and components, review batch records, track testing, sign documents, and validate all requirements for facilities, aseptic techniques, environmental monitoring, and deviation investigations. Our digital systems provide comprehensive batch record review and disposition, document review and approval, certificate of analysis review, and a review of analytical test methods. You can also enact environmental monitoring, aseptic gowning, and validation for all other processes, cleaning, and computerized systems.

Inspection Readiness

Our comprehensive audits will diagnose critical areas for improvement and serve as trial-runs that prepare you for your ofﬁcial audits and inspections.

Inspections can be very stressful at each stage of the product lifecycle, from pre-approval inspection to post-market product inspections. We’ll help you prepare for the U.S Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Medicines and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) and the European Medicines Agency — along with all other regulatory authority inspections. We provide trail-run internal and external audits, along with inspection readiness programs to support cGMP, GLP, or GCP operations. We offer gap analysis and follow-ups to previous inspections, along with preparation and training from industry experts familiar with the many demands of these inspections entail.

Internal & External Audits

cGMP Compliance

GLP Compliance
